Let $G$ be a finite group that acts on a set $X$. For each $g$ in $G$
let $X^g$ denote the set of elements in $X$ that are fixed by $g$. Then
the number of orbits $$|X/G| = \frac{1}{|G|} \sum_{g\in G} |X^g|.$$
